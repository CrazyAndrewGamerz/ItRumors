Netflix Builds Detailed Apple Store Replica

April 23, 2025

In a surprising move that has caught the attention of Apple fans and production designers alike, Netflix has built a highly detailed replica of an Apple Store for an upcoming film or television series. The replica, showcased in a series of behind-the-scenes shots, includes signature Apple aesthetics—clean lines, glass tables, and realistic product displays.

The set features product counters, a mock Genius Bar, and even promotional signage that closely mimics what one might see in a real Apple retail environment. While Netflix has not yet disclosed the title of the project, the level of detail suggests the store may play a central role in the story.

This level of authenticity is rarely seen outside of official Apple productions, fueling speculation about possible brand involvement—or at least close observation. The replica's purpose is still shrouded in mystery, but its execution has tech enthusiasts buzzing online.
